How it works

1
Buyer proves payment
The buyer's wallet/extension issues a cryptographic proof that funds were transferred.
2

Seller proves coins were sent to buyer's wallet — proof-of-delivery without middlemen.

Math verifies both proofs and triggers an automated burn of MrProve to record the settlement.
